Indulge yourself with some of most classic and popular cocktail drinks, some traditional, some with a twist. Can you find your favourite, or will you discover a new one?

Whiskey Sour

The simple and sophisticated drink. A sour whiskey that allows you to appriciate the flavours of the spirit. The added lemon gives a refreshing yet edgy sharpness.

Ingredients

Bourbon, Lemon juice, Sugar syrup, Egg white

Serving

Rocks glass

Method

Shaken, On the rocks

Original Mojito

A cocktail menu isn’t a cocktail menu without the one time favourite mojito. Originating from faraway Cuba, the famous drink has become one of the country’s cultural icons. Combining a traditional blend of lime and mint gives the classic mojito minty citrus base.

Ingredients

White rum, Soda water, Fresh lime juice, Sugar, Mint leaves

Serving

Highball glass

Method

On the rocks

Dragon Fruit Mojito

Put a delightful twist on the amazing classic mojito with a mouthwatering dragon fruit flavour. A unique ingredient of dragon fruit juice muddled together with mint, vanilla and limes for a fruity sparking mojito cocktail.

Ingredients

Coconut rum, Soda water, Dragonfruit, Lime juice, Syrup, Mint leaves

Serving

Highball glass

Method

On the rocks

Tequila Sunrise

Tequila sunrise is a summer trend in terms of cocktails. Give your body a boost of hydration with the subtly sour orange juice. The fresh taste of orange and berry makes the drink one of the greatest of modern classic cocktails.

Ingredients

Tequila, Orange juice, Grenadine

Serving

Highball glass

Method

On the rocks

Cosmopolitan

Add a little pink in your life with the famous 'Cosmo'. This city standard is a strong sweet-tart combination of citrus, balanced with a touch of cranberry and finished with flamed orange zest.

Ingredients

Vodka, Cointreau, Lime juice, Cranberry juice

Serving

Cocktail glass

Method

Straight up

Old Fashioned

One of the ultimate whiskey originals. Can you handle the combination of fresh, sweet and bitter. The muddling of sugar and bitters makes this a strong and smooth classic.

Ingredients

Bourbon, Angostura bitters, Sugar syrup

Serving

Rocks glass

Method

On the Rocks

Margarita

All the tequila lovers, step right up for the one time original. The fresh, crisp flavors of lime and salt creates the tart, zingy and satisfying taste associated with this mexican classic.

Ingredients

Tequila, Lime juice, Cointreau

Serving

Margarita glass

Method

Straight up, On the rocks or Frozen

Cherry & Rosemary Fizz

Mix up the original English Fizz by adding a hint of cherry and rosemary flavour to create a truly refreshing drink. The combination of cherry and lemon gives a very light, sour citrus flavor that allows the vodka to shine through.

Ingredients

Vodka, Cherry brandy, Lemon juice, Sugar syrup, Rosemary

Serving

Highball glass

Method

On the rocks

Champagne Cocktail

The Original classic champagne cocktail, and one of the oldest, well-known cocktail on record. Liven up the party with come bubbles and sweetness.

Ingredients

Champagne, Aromatic bitters, Cognac, Brown sugar cube

Serving

Champagne glass

Method

Straight up

French Martini

Introduce a little french and fruitiness to your martini with a hint of Chambord, fruity pineapple juice and blend of raspberries. A tasteful, sweetness in a little glass.

Ingredients

Vodka, Chambord, Pineapple juice

Serving

Cocktail glass

Method

Straight up

Sangria

Add a little spanish to your life with this burst of fruitiness in a jar. Go wild with fruity flavours to create this simple, traditional, perfect summer beverage.

Ingredients

Red wine, Orange juice, Lemonade, Fresh chopped fruit

Serving

Jar glass

Method

Straight up

Long Island Iced Tea

A classic cocktail sure to deliver a punch with its high combination of spirits. With a higher alcohol concentration than any other, the cola and lemon disguise this with a bitter, iced tea resemblance.

Ingredients

Vodka, Rum, Gin, Tequila, Triple sec, Sour mix, Cola, Lemon

Serving

Highball glass

Method

On the rocks
